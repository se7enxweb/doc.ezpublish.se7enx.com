At any time you can modify an existing Content item.

 

After you create a Content item, at any time it is possible to change its properties in Edit mode. You can edit content through relevant editing options.

 

 

 

 

Editing content

 

 

1. Go to the Content mode.

2. From the Content tree, open a Content item you would like to edit.

3. Click the Edit button in the Action bar.

The selected content is ready for editing.

 

 

 

 

Copying content

 

 

1. From the Content tree, open a content item you would like to copy.

2. Click the Copy button in the Action bar.

The Universal Discovery Widget will open.

3. Go to the location you want to copy the page to and click Confirm Selection.

A copy of the page will be created in the selected place in the Content tree.

 

 

 

 

Moving content

 

 

1. From the Content tree, open a content item you would like to move.

2. Click the Move button in the Action bar.

The Universal Discovery Widget will open.

3. Go to the location you want to move the page to and click Confirm Selection.

The page will be moved to the selected place in the Content tree (and deleted from the original location).
