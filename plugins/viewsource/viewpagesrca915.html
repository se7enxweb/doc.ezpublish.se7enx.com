Logging in

 

 

1. Go to http://[uri_of_ez]/ez in your web browser.

2. Enter your user name and password, which you specified during the installation process.

3. Click Login.

After you log in the first page you see is the Dashboard.

 

 

Logging out

 

To log out from the system click Logout in the upper right corner of the screen (under your user name).

The login window appears again after logging out.
